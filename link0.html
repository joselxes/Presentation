  • What is Tuva

    • Is a federal subject of Russia. The Tuvan republic lies at the geographical center of Asia, in southern Siberia.

  • Geography

    • The republic is situated in the far south of Siberia. Its capital city of Kyzyl is located near the geographic "center of Asia". The eastern part of the republic is forested and elevated, and the west is a drier lowland.

  • Religion

    • Two religions are widespread among the people of Tuva: Tibetan Buddhism and shamanism. Tibetan Buddhism's present-day spiritual leader is Tenzin.
